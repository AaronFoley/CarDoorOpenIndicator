title=Car Door Open Indicator
description=[h1]Description[/h1]
description=
description=Adds a simple flashing and beeping alert when any Door/Trunk/Hood is open whilst in the drivers seat of a car.
description=
description=[b]Features[/b]
description=- Adds Radial menu options to close Doors, the Trunk and Hood when the car is stopped
description=- You can right click the Engine/Door/Trunk icons to mute the beeping
description=
description=[h1]FAQ[/h1]
description=
description=[b]Is this safe to add to an already started game[/b]
description=Yes
description=[hr][/hr]
description=[b]Does this work with vehicle mod 'x'[/b]
description=Yes this should work with any Vehicle mod that is using normal vehicle part names.
description=Vehicles that have more than 1 Trunk or Hood, may only be triggered when the first object is open.
description=Doors only trigger when they are directly tied to a seat.
description=[hr][/hr]
description=[b]What happens if the doors are missing[/b]
description=If the door is missing then the alert will be triggered.
